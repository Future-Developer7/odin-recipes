Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Description

This grilled chicken sandwich is juicy, flavorful, and perfect for a hearty lunch. It's made with tender chicken breast, crisp veggies, and your favorite sauces stacked between fresh bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless chicken breast
  • Salt, pepper, and paprika to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 slices of bread or a sandwich bun
  • 1 slice of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise or garlic sauce

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breast with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and grill the chicken until fully cooked (about 5-7 minutes per side).
  3. Toast the bread or sandwich bun if desired.
  4. Spread mayonnaise on the bread.
  5. Layer lettuce, tomato, grilled chicken, and cheese on the bottom slice.
  6. Cover with the top slice of bread and press lightly.
  7. Cut in half and serve hot with fries or salad.
