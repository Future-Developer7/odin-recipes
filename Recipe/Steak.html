A Well-Cooked Steak

Description

This recipe shows you how to make a juicy, flavorful steak at home using simple ingredients and a hot pan. It’s a perfect dish to impress your family or enjoy on a special night!

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye or sirloin steak (1-inch thick)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary or thyme (optional)

Steps

  1. Let the steak rest at room temperature for 20–30 minutes before cooking.
  2. Pat it dry and season both sides with salt and black pepper.
  3. Heat a cast-iron skillet on high until very hot.
  4. Add olive oil and place the steak in the pan. Sear for 2–3 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  5. Add butter, garlic, and herbs. Spoon melted butter over the steak for extra flavor.
  6. Remove from pan and rest for 5 minutes before slicing. Serve hot!
