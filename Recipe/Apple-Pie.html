Apple Pie

Description

Apple pie is a timeless dessert made with sweet, cinnamon-spiced apples wrapped in a buttery, flaky crust. It's the perfect comfort food, often served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (cold, cut into cubes)
  • 6–8 tablespoons ice water
  • 6–7 apples (peeled, cored, and sliced)
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (for filling)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. To make the crust, mix flour and butter until crumbly, then add ice water slowly until dough forms. Divide in two and chill for 30 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, toss apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice.
  4. Roll out one portion of dough and place in a pie dish. Add apple filling.
  5. Roll out second dough and cover the pie. Crimp edges and cut slits in the top for steam to escape.
  6. Brush the top with beaten egg.
  7. Bake for 45–50 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.
  8. Cool slightly before serving. Enjoy warm or chilled.
