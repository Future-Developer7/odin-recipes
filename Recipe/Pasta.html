Creamy Pasta Recipe

Creamy Pasta

Description

This creamy pasta dish is rich, comforting, and easy to make. It's perfect for lunch or dinner and can be customized with your favorite veggies or protein.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté the garlic for about 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until melted and smooth.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then garnish with parsley if desired.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
