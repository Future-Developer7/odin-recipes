Mango Custard

Description

Mango custard is a smooth, creamy dessert made with fresh mangoes and vanilla custard. It's perfect for hot days and is loved by both kids and adults. Serve chilled for the best experience!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes (peeled and chopped)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp custard powder (vanilla flavor)
  • 3 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Chopped fruits or dry fruits for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix custard powder with ¼ cup cold milk to make a smooth paste.
  2. Heat the remaining milk in a pan and bring it to a gentle boil.
  3. Add sugar and stir well until dissolved.
  4. Slowly add the custard paste while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  5. Cook on low heat until the custard thickens (about 4–5 minutes).
  6. Let the custard cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until chilled.
  7. Blend one mango into a puree and mix into the chilled custard. Add chopped mango pieces too.
  8. Serve cold, topped with more mango cubes or nuts if desired.
