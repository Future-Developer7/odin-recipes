Choco-lava Cake

Description

Choco-lava cake is a rich, gooey dessert with a soft cake exterior and molten chocolate center. Perfect for chocolate lovers, this indulgent treat is best served warm and fresh out of the oven.

Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate (chopped)
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F). Grease 4 ramekins or cupcake molds.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together using a microwave or double boiler. Let cool slightly.
  3. In a bowl, beat eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick and pale.
  4. Gently fold the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture.
  5. Add flour and mix until just combined. Add vanilla if using.
  6. Pour the batter into greased ramekins.
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes until the edges are set but the center is soft.
  8. Let cool for 1 minute, then invert onto plates and serve immediately.
