Healthy Salad

Description

This refreshing garden salad is light, crisp, and full of healthy vegetables. Perfect as a starter or a light meal, it combines crunchy greens with a tangy homemade dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/2 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/4 red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop lettuce, halve the cherry tomatoes, slice the cucumber and onion, and shred the carrots.
  3. In a large bowl, combine all vegetables.
  4. In a small bowl, mix olive oil and lemon juice/vinegar. Add salt and pepper.
  5. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.
  6. Serve immediately for best taste and crunch.
